Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my stencil to my device?

After generating your stencil, tap the \"Share\" button. On iOS, you'll be prompted to allow photo library access. Once granted, your stencil will be saved directly to your Photos app, ready to export to Procreate or print.

What's the difference between Light, Medium, and Heavy stencils?

Light: Clean outlines only, perfect for simple designs. Medium: Adds texture and contour lines using cross-hatching and stippling. Heavy: Includes shading with solid black fills for maximum depth and contrast.

Can I use this app on my iPad?

Yes! Body Bound is fully optimized for iPad. Generate stencils and export them directly to Procreate or your favorite design app.

Why does my stencil look different from the original photo?

Our AI converts realistic photos into line art suitable for tattooing. The stencil maintains the exact proportions and composition while simplifying details into clean lines that transfer well to skin.

Is my photo data stored or shared?

Your privacy is important to us. Photos are processed securely and are not stored permanently on our servers. We do not share your images with third parties.
