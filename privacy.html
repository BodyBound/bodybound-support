Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Summary: We respect your privacy. Your photos are processed securely and are not stored permanently. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

Introduction

Body Bound Stencil Generator (\"we,\" \"our,\" or \"us\") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Photos: Images you upload or capture for stencil conversion. These are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app optimization and crash reporting.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve app performance and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Convert your photos into tattoo stencils
  • Save generated stencils to your device's photo library (with your permission)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Provide customer support when requested

Photo Processing

When you submit a photo for stencil conversion:

  • The image is sent securely to our AI processing service
  • The stencil is generated and returned to your device
  • Images are not stored permanently on our servers
  • We do not use your photos for training AI models
  • We do not share your photos with third parties

Data Storage

Generated stencils can be saved locally on your device in our in-app gallery. This data remains on your device and is not synced to external servers. You can delete saved stencils at any time from within the app.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: For image-to-stencil conversion (images processed securely)
  • Expo/React Native: App development framework

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture photos for stencil conversion
  • Photo Library (Read): To select reference images
  • Photo Library (Write): To save generated stencils

You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the \"Last Updated\" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us at:

support@bodybound.app
